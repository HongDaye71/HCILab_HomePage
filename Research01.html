Research

HCI (Human-Computer Interaction)

The Human-Computer Interaction Laboratory is studying computer-based technologies such as virtual reality, artificial intelligence, robots, and automated vehicle, and human behavior, cognition, emotion, brain, and bio-signals.These studies are conducted in two main directions, First, we want to study and help human physical and mental problems using human-computer interaction or computer technology.Second, we study the direction in which virtual reality, artificial intelligence, robot, and automobile technology should go based on our understanding of humans.

Serious Games

Serial Game refers to a game designed for a specific purpose rather than entertainment. Functional games are being studied and developed in a variety of ways, including health, promotion, education, management, medical care, advertising, and welfare.Among them, the human-computer interaction laboratory mainly studies health functional games, educational functional games, and therapeutic functional games.The picture below is a scene from a presentation practice game for students and an emotional cognitive training game for those who are not familiar with human emotions.

Medical Software and Others

The human-computer interaction laboratory is researching and developing methodologies to induce changes in human cognition, emotion, and behavior and improve the quality of life through them.The picture below shows the execution screen of the medical software currently under development and various equipment under study.
