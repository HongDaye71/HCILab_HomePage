Lecture

Department of Computer Science is aimed toward producing advanced key human resources in the field of computer engineering and computer science, which are the foundation of future IT technology, and contributing to the national competitiveness of Korea. The full-time faculty of the department is comprised of 25 experts in the field of computer engineering and computer science, and the teaching staff has formed highly intensive research groups in fields such as computer systems, databases, networks, algorithms, artificial intelligence, and image processing.
